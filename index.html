onTime! is a smart app that will help you schedule your studying time to submit all assignments on time! It is an easy and extremly handy tool.
You may set up notifications, various days and time when you are ready to work on your assignments.
If you skip one day of study, just let onTime! know and it will reschedule all calendar for you. If you lack of time to fulfill all tasks onTime! will jelp you to be most productive depends on priorities.

onTime! is created by students for students of any ages and it is absolutely free app!
